Our Story

Founded in 2015, Tickle Treats Bakery began as a small family-owned shop with a passion for creating delicious, handcrafted pastries. What started as a humble kitchen experiment quickly grew into a beloved neighborhood bakery.

Our founder, Ananya Mehta, brought generations-old recipes from her grandmother's kitchen in Mumbai, blending them with modern techniques to create our signature treats. Today, we continue this tradition of excellence, using only the finest ingredients sourced from local farmers and suppliers.

At Tickle Treats, we believe baking is an art form. Each morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to prepare fresh batches of our specialties, ensuring every bite delivers the perfect balance of flavor and texture.
